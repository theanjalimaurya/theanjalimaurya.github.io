Hello, I am Anjali Maurya and, I am a UI/UX designer based in Berlin originally hail from India.

Drawing is most definitely my first creative love. I lose track of time when I'm drawing, crafting, backpacking over mountains, or eating food in new countries I explore. I love connecting and understanding people. I get jazzed up leading in-depth user research and brainstorming solutions. I'm always trying to grow and learn something new.

On completing my M.B.A in Human Resource, I joined as a Human Resource recruiter in an IT company with a focus on managing full cycle recruitment, from identifying potential hires to interviewing and evaluating candidates. During, this period I recruited for almost every profile like developers, testers, and designers. By this point, I do have a fleeting interest in UI/UX Design and had spent time reading articles. I was speaking to and recruiting some of the best digital talents around. I was able to ask them questions, see their portfolios and build deep relationships. My interest was piqued, and I had decided that I wanted to transform my career and become a UI/UX Designer.

I’m currently available for both full-time and freelance work in Berlin or remotely. Feel free to connect with me!
